# GTI V1 Premium Microcopy & Pitch

## 1. Landing Page
- **Headline**: Touring Intelligence for the Modern Artist.
- **Subheadline**: GTI transforms fragmented settlement data into a unified operating system for global tours.

## 2. Feature Descriptions
- **Tour Health**: Real-time performance metrics and market-by-market pacing analysis.
- **Roadmap**: A centralized timeline for logistics, venue metadata, and geographic context.
- **Wallet**: Transparent financial waterfalls and net payout projections for every show.
- **Admin Portal**: Secure ingestion and AI-powered parsing of raw settlement reports.

## 3. Tooltip Definitions
- **Sell-through**: The percentage of total ticket inventory sold relative to venue capacity.
- **Pacing**: The daily velocity of ticket sales compared to historical tour averages.
- **Cold Zone**: Markets performing below target thresholds requiring immediate marketing intervention.
- **Net Take-Home (Estimated)**: Projected artist earnings after venue fees, production costs, and commissions.
- **Confidence**: The statistical reliability of a data point based on the source and completeness of the report.

## 4. Action Labels
- **Boost Market**: Initiate Marketing Push
- **Add Note**: Append Internal Note
- **Flag**: Flag for Review
- **Publish**: Commit to Profile

## 5. The Thesis: Engineering Transparency
**The Friction**: Touring is the lifeblood of the music industry, yet it remains plagued by data opacity. Artists and managers often wait weeks for fragmented PDF settlements, leaving them blind to real-time market performance and true profitability.

**The OS**: GTI is the first artist-centric touring operating system. We bridge the gap between raw data and actionable intelligence, providing a single source of truth for the entire touring team.

**The Foundation**: V1 establishes the "Historical-First" foundation. We ingest unstructured settlement reports and transform them into a structured Roadmap and a financial Wallet. We identify "Cold Zones" early enough to save them.

**The Horizon**: The next phase of GTI introduces real-time ticketing API integrations for live pacing, a centralized offers inbox to streamline the booking process, and automated radius clause monitoring.
